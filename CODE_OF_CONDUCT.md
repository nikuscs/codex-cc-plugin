# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, direct, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Keep technical disagreements factual
- Respect other contributors' time

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Deliberate disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and discussions that do not meet this standard.

For conduct concerns, open a private issue or contact the maintainer through the repository profile.
